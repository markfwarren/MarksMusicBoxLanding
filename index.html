What's a Mark's Music Box?

This is a "boutique drum kit" app made for fun and getting ideas for music composition. A great time for pro and novice alike, it focuses on specially designed and curated sonic kits rather than you're run of the mill, stock drum kit. There are, of course, your basic kick, snare and high hat. But also included are loops, samples and synth lines!.
